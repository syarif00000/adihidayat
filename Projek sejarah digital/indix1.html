About Us

Lembaga Dakwah Kampus is a non-profit organization that focuses on spreading the message of Islam among university students.

Our dedicated team of volunteers organizes various activities, including lectures, workshops, and social programs, to foster a positive environment for learning and practicing Islam on campus.

Our Services

  • Islamic Education

    We provide Islamic education programs, including Qur'an classes, Islamic lectures, and workshops on Islamic topics.

  • Community Outreach

    We engage in community outreach efforts, such as charity events, blood donation drives, and volunteering activities to promote social welfare.

  • Da'wah Campaigns

    We organize da'wah campaigns to raise awareness about Islam and invite others to learn about its principles and teachings.
